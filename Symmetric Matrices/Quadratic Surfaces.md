Say we have a constant $C$
# Curves in $\mathbb{R}^2$
$Q(x)=x^TAx$ where $A\in\mathbb{R}^{2 \times2}$. Then,
$$C=x^TAx$$
Is a curve in $\mathbb{R}^2$.
Say $A=\b{2&1\\1&2}$. Then $Q(x)=2x^2+2y^2+2xy=C$.
