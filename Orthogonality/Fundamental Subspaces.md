# $\text{Row}A$
The space spanned by the rows of matrix.

## Properties
- The basis for $\text{Row}A$ is given by the pivot rows of $A$.
- $\dim{(\text{Row}A)} = \dim{(\text{Col}A)}$ 
- $\text{Row} A = {\text{Col} A}^T$
